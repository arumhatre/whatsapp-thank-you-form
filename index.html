What is Chronic Obesity?

Chronic obesity is a silent threat to your health. Living with chronic obesity can put you at risk for many lifelong health risks. Chronic obesity typically refers to cases where the BMI range is above 40 and is consistently in the Obesity Class II or III range over an extended period, indicating severe or morbid obesity.
